Privacy Policy

Media keys are for music is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your information when you use our Google Chrome extension.

1. Information Collection
We do not collect any personally identifiable information. Our extension does not require or request access to your personal data beyond what is necessary for its basic functionality.

2. Use of Data
The extension does not sell, share, or rent any user data to third parties.

All browsing or interaction history processed by the extension is automatically deleted within 120 seconds of processing.

No data is stored in any external database or persistent storage system.

3. Data Retention
Temporary data used for providing functionality (such as session history or interaction logs) is stored locally and ephemerally.

This data is automatically cleared within 120 seconds, ensuring your information is not retained beyond its immediate use.

4. Third-Party Services
The extension does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data.

No data is shared with advertisers, analytics platforms, or external APIs that could access your personal or browsing information.

5. Security
We follow best practices to ensure that your data is processed securely and is never stored or transmitted beyond your local device.

6. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
